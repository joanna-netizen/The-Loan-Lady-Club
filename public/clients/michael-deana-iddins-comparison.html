Why Scenario C Works Hardest for You

Your $225,000 is sitting in a brokerage account earning a return that may not outpace the cost of carrying more mortgage. Putting more into the purchase uses that money at a rate of 6.375% and immediately reduces what you owe every month. That is a guaranteed return in the form of a lower payment and less interest over the life of the loan.
