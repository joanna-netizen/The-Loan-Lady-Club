What Changes Between These Scenarios

Moving your down payment from $400,000 to $500,000 lowers your estimated monthly payment by about $623.87, while increasing the estimated cash needed at closing by about $98,929.17.

Each scenario also carries a slightly different lender fee total and prepaid interest amount, since those figures are tied to the loan amount and the date of closing.

All figures shown are rough estimates for comparison purposes only, based on the initial scenario provided. Your actual rate, payment, and costs could be higher once a full Loan Estimate is issued.
