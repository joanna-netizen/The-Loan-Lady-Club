‡ Understanding the DPA PRO Second Loan (Options F & G)

Unlike CalHFA's second loan — which is deferred and has no monthly payment — the DPA PRO second loan requires a monthly payment. It's structured as a 10-year fixed loan at a rate 2% higher than your first mortgage rate. Think of it like a small car payment that goes away after 10 years.

For Option F: $189/mo for 10 years, then it's gone.  |  For Option G: $272/mo for 10 years, then it's gone.

Both monthly payment totals shown on the cards already include this second loan payment — so what you see is your true all-in monthly housing cost.
