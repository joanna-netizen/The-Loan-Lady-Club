With Option A, CalHFA covers the down payment so the only money you need to bring to closing is the small remaining balance after the seller credit is applied. The trade-off is a slightly higher rate and a layered second lien. With Option B, you bring your own down payment along with remaining costs not covered by the seller credit, but the rate is meaningfully lower and the loan structure is simpler. Neither path is wrong. The right one depends on where your savings sit today and what feels right for your family.
