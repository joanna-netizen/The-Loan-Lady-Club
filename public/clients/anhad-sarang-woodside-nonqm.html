Of the $23,905 in total closing costs, $7,560 represents discount points (1.5% of the loan amount). Non-QM programs carry higher points than conventional financing as a structural feature of bank statement lending. This is a reasonable starting point for a seller credit conversation with the builder. Points and third-party fees together represent approximately $16,585 in costs the builder could partially offset through concessions. All figures are estimates based on the scenario provided.
Property taxes reflected in this scenario use a total effective rate of 1.58%, which includes the base county tax rate of 1.16% plus the Mello Roos special assessment applicable to this Woodside community. Mello Roos is a builder-imposed district tax common in new construction developments. It is a fixed cost of this property and is included in the monthly payment and escrow figures shown.
